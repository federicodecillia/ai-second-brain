# People

Contacts live here, grouped by relationship type. Create subfolders as you accumulate real contacts (anti-overcomplication: do not pre-create empty ones).

Suggested subfolders:
- `clients/` — people you do paid work for
- `prospects/` — potential clients
- `collaborators/` — partners, peers
- `suppliers/` — vendors, tools, subcontractors

Rule: every contact links to at least one area or project (and vice versa). Use `03_Resources/templates/person.md`.
